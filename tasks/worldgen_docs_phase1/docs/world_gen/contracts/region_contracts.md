# DATA CONTRACT — REGION CONTRACTS

## PlateRecord

```json
{
  "plateId": "plate_01",
  "plateClass": "continental",
  "seedPoint": {
    "x": 42,
    "y": 17
  },
  "upliftBias": 0.71,
  "fractureBias": 0.43,
  "compressionBias": 0.68,
  "driftBias": 0.57,
  "arcFormationBias": 0.36
}
```

### Notes
- `seedPoint` is populated by `TectonicSkeletonGenerator.plateSeedDistribution`.
- Bias fields may be neutral placeholders during plate seed distribution and must not be read as generated uplift, motion, fracture, compression, or arc data until their dedicated tectonic passes run.
- Plate motion generated by `TectonicSkeletonGenerator.plateMotionVectors` stays in an intermediate output and is not written back into `PlateRecord`.
- `fractureMaskField` is emitted as a separate tectonic field and must not be backfilled into `PlateRecord.fractureBias`.

## ContinentRecord

```json
{
  "continentId": "cont_01",
  "nameSeed": "culture_group_a",
  "macroShape": "crescent_mass",
  "plateIds": ["plate_01"],
  "reliefRegionIds": ["relief_03", "relief_04"],
  "climateBandIds": ["climate_02", "climate_03"],
  "primaryReliefRegionId": "relief_03",
  "primaryClimateBandId": "climate_02"
}
```

### Notes
- `ContinentRecord` remains the finalized physical-record target and still requires resolved `reliefRegionIds`, `climateBandIds`, `primaryReliefRegionId`, and `primaryClimateBandId`.
- `ReliefElevationGenerator.continentBodies` may emit `recordDraft` objects aligned with this shape, but those drafts are intentionally incomplete until later relief/climate linkage microsteps fill the pending references.

## SeaRegionRecord

```json
{
  "seaRegionId": "sea_03",
  "basinType": "labyrinth_sea",
  "stormPressure": 0.61,
  "navigability": 0.57,
  "climateBandIds": ["climate_04", "climate_05"],
  "primaryClimateBandId": "climate_04"
}
```

### Notes
- `SeaRegionRecord` remains the finalized physical-record target and still requires resolved climate references plus preliminary navigability/storm summaries.
- `HydrosphereGenerator.seaRegionClusters` may emit `recordDraft` objects aligned with this shape, but those drafts are intentionally incomplete until later climate-linkage and navigability microsteps fill the pending fields.
- `HydrosphereGenerator.seaNavigabilityTagging` may emit enriched draft objects where `stormPressure` and `navigability` are already filled, while `climateBandIds` and `primaryClimateBandId` remain intentionally pending.
- During hydrosphere inland-sea formation, draft `basinType` may already be specialized to values such as `inland_sea` or `semi_enclosed_sea`, while storm/climate/navigation semantics remain unresolved.

## RiverBasinRecord

```json
{
  "riverBasinId": "basin_01",
  "basinType": "exorheic",
  "sourceMountainSystemIds": ["mnt_01"],
  "reliefRegionIds": ["relief_09", "relief_10"],
  "climateBandIds": ["climate_03"],
  "terminalSeaRegionIds": ["sea_03"],
  "primaryReliefRegionId": "relief_09",
  "primaryClimateBandId": "climate_03",
  "catchmentScale": 0.78,
  "basinContinuity": 0.71
}
```

Hydrosphere ingestion note:
- `watershedSegmentation` may emit `recordDraft` payloads aligned to this contract, but those drafts are not finalized `riverBasins[]`.
- Drafts can include provisional `terminalSeaRegionIds` from `seaRegionClusters`; climate-band and source-mountain linkage must remain pending until later hydrology/climate steps.
- Migration note: this is additive pre-record preparation only and does not change `RiverBasinRecord` required keys or final package semantics.

River system scaffold note:
- `RiverSystemGenerator` now emits hydrology-stage `riverBasins[]` records from contract-valid watershed drafts and keeps incomplete drafts deferred with diagnostics.
- `flowAccumulationField` informs record-link metadata, but the record itself stays a physical summary and does not encode river-routing graphs or local river placement.
- `deltaLakeMarshTagging` may provide structural delta/lake/marsh summaries keyed by watershed and `riverBasinIdHint`, but those tags do not mutate `RiverBasinRecord` keys and do not represent final lake systems, marsh biomes, or river-delta systems.
- `majorRiverCandidates` may provide macro-scale source-to-mouth line candidates keyed by watershed and `riverBasinIdHint`, but those candidates remain analysis output and do not mutate `RiverBasinRecord` keys.
- `climateBandIds` and `primaryClimateBandId` remain present in the record shape but may be empty until the later climate blend step; this avoids inventing climate-band truth during hydrology-only output.
- Final climate linkage, source mountain linkage, route graph construction, local river placement, final river deltas, lake hydrology simulation, marsh biome construction, settlement logic, and gameplay semantics remain future microsteps.
- Migration note: validation semantics changed additively for hydrology-stage output. `RiverBasinRecord` keeps the same keys, but climate references are no longer required to be non-empty before climate blend.

## ClimateBandRecord

```json
{
  "climateBandId": "climate_03",
  "bandType": "humid_temperate",
  "reliefRegionIds": ["relief_09", "relief_10"],
  "seaRegionIds": ["sea_03"],
  "primaryReliefRegionId": "relief_09",
  "temperatureBias": 0.58,
  "humidityBias": 0.67,
  "seasonalityBias": 0.44
}
```

Climate envelope partial-output note:
- `ClimateEnvelopeGenerator` now declares the future dependency surface for `ClimateBandRecord` output, including relief/elevation, hydrosphere, river-system, and marine-carving context.
- The current partial runtime emits `latitudeBandBaselineField`, `prevailingWindField`, `humidityTransportField`, `temperatureColdLoadField`, `stormCorridorField`, `coastalDecayBurdenField`, `seasonalityField`, `climateZoneClassification`, `climateBands[]`, `regionalClimateSummaries`, `rainShadowEffect`, and rain-shadow-adjusted `wetnessField`.
- `latitudeBandBaselineField`, `temperatureColdLoadField`, `stormCorridorField`, `coastalDecayBurdenField`, `seasonalityField`, and `wetnessField` are baseline inputs for climate-band, route-risk, isolation, biome-envelope, and coastal pressure/history work, not biome-envelope output by themselves.
- `seasonalityField.summary.regionalSummary` may carry coarse regional seasonality buckets, while `regionalClimateSummaries` is the standalone summary-table output for emitted climate-band records by relief region, continent body, and sea-region cluster.
- `climateZoneClassification.zoneSummaries` may carry coarse connected climate-zone summaries and record-compatibility diagnostics, but that intermediate output does not change `ClimateBandRecord` keys.
- `ClimateBandRecord` shape and validation semantics remain unchanged in this microstep.

## ReliefRegionRecord

```json
{
  "reliefRegionId": "relief_09",
  "reliefType": "plateau",
  "plateIds": ["plate_01"],
  "continentIds": ["cont_01"],
  "adjacentSeaRegionIds": ["sea_03"],
  "primaryPlateId": "plate_01",
  "elevationBias": 0.74,
  "ruggednessBias": 0.46,
  "coastalInfluence": 0.28
}
```

### Notes
- `ReliefRegionRecord` is now produced by `ReliefElevationGenerator.reliefRegions` for large connected mountain, plateau, plain, basin, and coastal-belt regions.
- Runtime extraction fills `plateIds`, optional `continentIds`, `primaryPlateId`, and normalized elevation/ruggedness/coastal descriptors from Phase 1 physical fields.
- `adjacentSeaRegionIds` may remain empty until later sea-region extraction/linkage exists.
- Relief-region records must not encode climate classification, local biome placement, terrain cells, river routing, or gameplay semantics.
